Lasagna

Italian Cuisine

Prep time: 40 minutes

Cook time: 1 hour

Servings: 8 pieces

No need to wait for a special occasion to enjoy a rich, layered lasagna. Here's how you can make this classic dish at home with authentic Italian flavors.

Ingredients

  • 12 sheets of lasagna pasta
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 800g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 250g ricotta cheese
  • 200g mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, grated

Margherita Pizza

Italian Cuisine

Prep time: 20 minutes

Cook time: 15 minutes

Servings: 1 large pizza

A classic Italian pizza with a crispy thin crust, fresh tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and basil. Simple yet absolutely delicious.

Ingredients

  • 250g pizza dough
  • 200g canned San Marzano tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 200g fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt to taste
