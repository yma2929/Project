🌍 Middle Eastern Recipes

Here are some of the most popular Middle Eastern recipes that you can try at home:

🍗Kabsa (Saudi Arabian Spiced Rice with Chicken)

  • 📗Medium
  • ⏳1hr and 15 mins
  • 👥4-6

📜Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice (soaked for 30 minutes)
  • 1 whole chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 2 onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 large carrot (grated)
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup raisins (optional)
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1/4 cup slivered almonds (toasted)

🌶Spices:

  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp coriander

📖Instructions:

  • 1️⃣ Heat oil in a large pot and sauté onions until golden brown.
  • 2️⃣ Add garlic, tomatoes, and tomato paste. Cook until tomatoes soften.
  • 3️⃣ Add chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned.
  • 4️⃣ Stir in all the spices and cook for another 2 minutes.
  • 5️⃣ Pour in chicken broth and bring to a boil. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  • 6️⃣ Remove chicken, grill or roast it separately for a crispy texture.
  • 7️⃣ Add rice, grated carrot, and raisins to the broth. Cook until rice absorbs liquid.
  • 8️⃣ Cover and cook on low heat for 15 minutes.
  • 9️⃣ Fluff the rice and serve with grilled chicken, almonds, and extra raisins.

🌯Shawarma (Levantine Spiced Meat Wraps)

  • 📗Medium
  • ⏳45 minutes
  • 👥4

📜Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken breast or beef (thinly sliced)
  • 3 tbsp yogurt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)

🌶Spices:

  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp paprika

📖Instructions:

  • 1️⃣ Mix all marinade ingredients and coat the meat. Marinate for at least 2 hours.
  • 2️⃣ Heat a pan or grill and cook meat until browned and cooked through.
  • 3️⃣ Serve in pita bread with fresh veggies and garlic sauce..

🥗Fattoush (Levantine Bread Salad)

  • 🟢Easy
  • ⏳15 minutes
  • 👥4

📜Ingredients:

  • 1 small head of romaine lettuce (chopped)
  • 1 large cucumber (diced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup radishes (thinly sliced)
  • 1/2 cup green onions (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley (chopped)
  • 1 pita bread (toasted and broken into pieces)

🌶Dressings:

  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pomegranate molasses (optional)
  • 1 tsp sumac
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 3 tbsp olive oil

📖Instructions:

  • 1️⃣ In a bowl, mix all the salad ingredients.
  • 2️⃣ In a small bowl, whisk together the dressing ingredients.
  • 3️⃣ Pour the dressing over the salad and toss well.
  • 4️⃣ Add toasted pita chips just before serving for crunch.

🥯Manakish (Za’atar Flatbread)

  • 🟢Easy
  • ⏳1 hour
  • 👥6-8 flatbreads

📜Ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp yeast
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 3 tbsp olive oil

🌶Topping:

  • 1/4 cup za’atar
  • 1/4 cup olive oil

📖Instructions:

  • 1️⃣ In a bowl, mix yeast, sugar, and warm water. Let it sit for 10 minutes.
  • 2️⃣ Add flour, salt, and olive oil. Knead into a soft dough. Let rise for 1 hour.
  • 3️⃣ Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F).
  • 4️⃣ Divide dough into small circles and flatten.
  • 5️⃣ Mix za’atar with olive oil and spread over the dough.
  • 6️⃣ Bake for 10-12 minutes until golden.

🍪Ma’amoul (Middle Eastern Date-Filled Cookies)

  • 📗Medium
  • ⏳1 hour
  • 👥20 cookies

📜Dough Ingredients:

  • 3 cups semolina flour
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup butter (melted)
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 cup warm milk
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Filling:

  • 1 1/2 cups date paste
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp butter

📖Instructions:

  • 1️⃣ Mix semolina, flour, sugar, and baking powder. Add melted butter and rub into the flour.
  • 2️⃣ Gradually add warm milk and knead into a soft dough. Let rest for 30 minutes.
  • 3️⃣ Mix date paste, cinnamon, and butter.
  • 4️⃣ Divide dough into small balls, flatten, and add date filling. Seal and shape into patterns.
  • 5️⃣ Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 15-20 minutes until golden.
